Policies

Payment Policy

Flat prices, simple payment, no hidden charges.

How We Price Jobs

Every quote from GarageClean is a flat number — not an hourly rate, not an estimate with asterisks. You know the price before we touch anything. If the actual scope on arrival is meaningfully different from what was described, we'll discuss it before we start, not after.

Ways to Pay

  • Credit and debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Interac e-transfer

We don't store card numbers or payment details after a transaction is processed.

When Payment Is Due

Standard jobs are paid in full when the crew wraps up and you've had a chance to look things over. For larger premium or estate projects, we require a deposit upfront to lock in your date — that amount comes straight off the final invoice, so you're not paying more overall.

Late or Failed Payments

If a payment doesn't go through, we'll reach out right away. We ask that it gets resolved within 48 hours. Accounts with unresolved balances won't be eligible for rebooking until they're settled.

Refunds

Not happy with how a job went? Reach out within 24 hours and we'll make it right before we talk about money. Refunds on completed services are considered case by case and only issued when there's a clear failure within the agreed scope. Billing disputes need to be raised within 7 days of your invoice.

Tipping

Tips aren't expected or factored into our pricing — but if your crew did a great job and you want to show it, they'll appreciate it. Everything goes directly to the people who did the work.
