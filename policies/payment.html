Payment Policy

Accepted methods, timing, deposits, and refunds.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit and debit cards — Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • E-transfer

All transactions are processed securely. We do not store complete card details.

2. Payment Timing

Payment is due upon completion of service, unless otherwise agreed in writing. Large or estate jobs may require a deposit upfront — this is applied toward your final invoice.

3. Failed Payments

Failed or declined payments must be resolved within 48 hours. Unresolved non-payment may result in service suspension or cancellation of future bookings.

4. Deposits

Deposits required for large or estate jobs are non-refundable if cancelled outside the cancellation window. Prepaid services cancelled within policy may be credited toward a future booking.

5. Refunds & Disputes

  • Dissatisfied with your service? Contact us within 24 hours of completion and we'll make it right.
  • Refunds on completed services are issued only in cases of confirmed service failure.
  • Billing disputes must be raised within 7 days of your invoice date.

6. Tips

Gratuities are never expected but always appreciated — they go directly to your crew.
