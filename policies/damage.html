Damage Policy

Coverage, reporting requirements, and how claims are resolved.

1. Our Commitment

GarageClean is fully insured. We treat your property with care and take full responsibility for any accidental damage caused directly by our crew during a scheduled service.

2. What's Covered

  • Breakage caused by our team's actions
  • Scratches or cracks from our tools or equipment
  • Damage from improper product use by our crew

3. What's Not Covered

  • Pre-existing wear, damage, or deterioration present before our arrival
  • Fragile or improperly secured items not disclosed to us in advance
  • Electronics, unless we were specifically asked to handle them
  • Water or plumbing-related damage unrelated to our cleaning
  • Items of extraordinary value not disclosed prior to the job
  • Damage caused by factors outside our control

4. Reporting Damage

Any damage must be reported within 24 hours of service completion, with clear photos and a written description sent to us by email or text. Failure to report within this window may void coverage.

5. Resolution

We'll investigate all claims promptly. Resolution may include repair, replacement, or a service credit at our discretion — capped at the current market value of the item. Our total liability is capped at the cost of the service provided, unless otherwise required by law.

6. Client Responsibilities

To protect both parties, please secure or remove valuables and fragile items before we arrive, and let us know about any surfaces requiring special care. This helps us do our best work safely.
