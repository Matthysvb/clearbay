Damage Policy

We're fully insured. Here's how we handle it if something goes wrong.

We're Insured and We Take Responsibility

GarageClean carries full liability insurance on every job. If our crew breaks, scratches, or damages something that was in good condition before we arrived, we own it and we fix it. That's not a policy hedge — it's how we actually operate.

What We Cover

  • Anything our crew physically breaks, cracks, or scratches during the job
  • Damage from our equipment — dollies, carts, cleaning tools
  • Any staining or surface damage caused by a product we applied

What We Don't Cover

  • Damage or deterioration that was already present before we started
  • Fragile items that weren't disclosed to us — if it's delicate, tell us in advance so we can handle it accordingly
  • Electronics we weren't specifically asked to move or handle
  • Plumbing or structural issues unrelated to anything we did
  • Items of high value (jewelry, collectibles, artwork) that weren't flagged ahead of time

How to Report Damage

Spot something after we leave? Contact us within 24 hours of service completion. Send us a photo and a quick description by text or email. We take these seriously and respond fast — usually same day.

How We Resolve It

We'll review what happened and get back to you promptly. Depending on what was damaged, resolution could be repair, like-for-like replacement, or a credit toward a future service. Our liability is capped at the fair market value of the item and the total cost of the service — unless applicable law requires otherwise.

Help Us Help You

Before we arrive, please set aside or put away anything fragile, valuable, or irreplaceable. Point out surfaces that need special care. A two-minute heads-up at the start of the job saves a lot of trouble — and helps us do our best work safely in your space.
