Scope of Work Policy

What's included, what's not, and how we handle changes.

1. What's Included in Every Job

  • Complete garage empty-out — everything staged in your driveway
  • Dust and cobweb removal from walls, ceiling, joists, and all accessible surfaces
  • Full floor scrub and sweep
  • Shelf and storage unit wipe-down
  • Junk haul-away — disposal and donation handled for you
  • Full re-organization of everything you're keeping

2. Premium & Estate Packages Also Include

  • Inventory sorting and label systems
  • Shelving installation
  • Storage bins
  • Donation handling and coordination
  • Item valuation coordination (Estate only)
  • Storage coordination (Estate only)

3. Not Included in Standard Service

  • Mold remediation or biohazard cleanup
  • Hazardous materials removal (paint, fuel, chemicals)
  • Post-construction debris removal
  • Exterior garage cleaning or high-reach areas not safely accessible
  • Deep stain removal or floor restoration beyond standard cleaning

Need something not listed? Mention it when requesting your quote — we'll let you know if we can accommodate it and what it would cost.

4. Condition & Results

Results depend on the current condition of your garage. We cannot guarantee removal of permanent stains or damage beyond normal cleaning standards. Heavily soiled areas may require additional time or follow-up services.

5. Scope Changes

Any changes to scope during the job require approval, may incur additional charges, and depend on crew availability. Notify us as early as possible if your needs change.

6. Quality Assurance

If you have concerns about completed work, notify us within 24 hours of service completion. We'll review and re-clean affected areas within the original scope at no extra charge.
