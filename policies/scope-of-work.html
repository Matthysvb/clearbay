Policies

Scope of Work

Exactly what we do — and what falls outside a standard job.

What Every Job Includes

Regardless of package, here's what we show up and do:

  • Full empty-out — everything staged safely on your driveway
  • Walls, ceiling, and exposed joists swept and dusted top to bottom
  • Floor scrubbed and swept clear
  • All shelving, racks, and cabinet exteriors wiped down
  • Junk haul-away — we handle disposal and drop donations off ourselves
  • Everything you're keeping organized back into the space logically

What Premium and Estate Packages Add

Our higher-tier packages go further. Depending on what you've booked, that can include:

  • Dedicated inventory sorting and a label system so nothing gets lost again
  • New shelving units sourced and installed
  • Storage bins provided and loaded
  • Donation coordination — we handle the drop-off so you don't have to
  • Item valuation coordination for estate jobs where appraised value matters
  • Off-site storage coordination for anything you're not ready to part with

What We Don't Do

A few things fall outside what we can safely or legally handle:

  • Mold remediation and biohazard cleanup — these need certified contractors
  • Hazardous materials: paint, gasoline, propane, pesticides, or chemicals of any kind
  • Post-construction cleanup (drywall dust, framing debris)
  • Anything requiring work at heights beyond what's safely reachable
  • Floor restoration, epoxy, or stain removal beyond normal scrubbing

Have something unusual? Tell us upfront and we'll let you know honestly whether it fits or recommend someone who handles it.

Results Depend on Starting Condition

We can't reverse permanent staining, fix pre-existing cracks, or remove damage that was there before we arrived. What we can do is clean everything that's cleanable and organize everything that's organize-able. Years of built-up grime might take longer than an average job — we'll flag it if that changes your quote.

If the Scope Changes Mid-Job

Surprises happen — a garage that looked like a one-day job sometimes isn't. If the work goes beyond what was originally scoped, we'll pause, call you, and get approval before continuing. No surprises on your invoice.

Our Guarantee

If something within the agreed scope wasn't done to a reasonable standard, let us know within 24 hours of us leaving. We'll come back and sort it out — no charge, no argument.
