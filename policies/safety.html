Safety Policy

How we keep your home and our team safe on every job.

1. Our Safety Standards

Every GarageClean crew member is trained to work safely in residential environments. We follow established safety practices on every job to protect both your property and our team.

2. Hazardous Materials

Our team will not handle, move, or dispose of hazardous materials including but not limited to: gasoline, propane tanks, paint, pesticides, asbestos, or biohazardous waste. If such materials are present, we will notify you and may need to pause or modify the scope of work.

3. Right to Refuse

GarageClean reserves the right to decline or stop work if conditions are deemed unsafe — including structural instability, pest infestations, or the presence of hazardous materials. In such cases, you will not be charged for work not completed.

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Disclose any known safety hazards before the job begins
  • Ensure pets are secured and away from the work area
  • Provide safe, unobstructed access to the garage and utilities (water, electricity)
  • Inform us of any structural concerns or fragile areas

5. Equipment & Products

We use professional-grade cleaning equipment and products. Where possible, we select biodegradable and environmentally responsible options. If you have allergies or product sensitivities, please let us know before the job and we'll accommodate where possible.

6. Incidents

In the event of an injury or safety incident during a scheduled service, our team is trained to respond appropriately. Please contact us immediately if you witness a safety concern during your job.
