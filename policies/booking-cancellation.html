Booking & Cancellation Policy

How to book, cancel, or reschedule your service.

1. Booking

Services can be booked via our website, phone, or email, subject to availability and confirmation. Please provide accurate details about your garage size, condition, and any special requirements — inaccurate information may result in price adjustments or rescheduling. You'll receive a confirmation by email or text once your appointment is reserved.

2. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Cancellations and reschedule requests must be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a cancellation fee.
  • Same-day cancellations or no-shows may be charged up to $150 of the service cost.
  • Repeated no-shows may result in refusal of future bookings.
  • Rescheduling is subject to crew availability.

3. Lockouts & Access Issues

If our team cannot access your property due to no answer, incorrect instructions, or restricted entry, the visit may be treated as a cancellation and a lockout fee will apply.

4. Deposits

  • Deposits are applied toward your final service cost.
  • Deposits are non-refundable if you cancel outside the allowed cancellation window.
  • Prepaid services cancelled within policy may be credited toward a future booking.

5. If We Need to Cancel

We'll notify you as soon as possible. You may reschedule or receive a full refund or credit — your choice. Weather and emergencies permit rescheduling without penalty for both parties.

6. Policy Updates

By booking, you agree to this policy. The current version on our website applies to all future bookings.
