Booking & Cancellation

Simple rules so we both know what to expect.

Getting on the Schedule

Reach us through the quote form, email, or phone. Once we've talked through your garage and landed on a price, we'll confirm a date by text or email — that's when your spot is locked in. To quote you accurately, we need a realistic picture of size and condition. If the job turns out to be materially different from what was described, we'll flag it and talk before any work begins.

Cancelling or Moving Your Date

  • Cancel or reschedule with at least 48 hours' notice and there's no charge. We'll find you another date.
  • Under 24 hours' notice may result in a late cancellation fee of $75–$150 depending on job size.
  • Same-day no-shows forfeit any deposit held and may be charged a trip fee.
  • Clients with two or more no-shows may not be able to rebook.
  • Rescheduling always depends on crew availability — we'll do our best to fit you in quickly.

If Our Crew Can't Get In

We'll call and text if there's an access problem on arrival. If we can't reach you and can't get into the garage within a reasonable wait, the visit becomes a missed appointment and a lockout fee applies. Clear access instructions when you book save everyone the hassle.

How Deposits Work

Larger jobs — premium packages, estate projects, anything multi-day — require a deposit to hold your date. That deposit comes off your final invoice. Cancel within the policy window and it rolls over as a credit toward a future booking. Cancel late or no-show and the deposit is forfeited.

If We Have to Reschedule

We'll notify you the moment we know — weather events, illness, or anything unexpected. You get first choice of the next available date, and if you'd rather cancel entirely, your deposit comes back in full, no questions asked.

Questions?

This is a small local operation — if something doesn't fit neatly into the above, just reach out and we'll work it out person to person.
