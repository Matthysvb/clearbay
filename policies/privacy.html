Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a quote or contact us, we collect information you provide — including your name, phone number, email address, and property address. We use this solely to provide and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your quote request and schedule your service
  • To communicate about your booking (confirmation, reminders, follow-up)
  • To improve our service quality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Payment processing is handled securely — we do not store complete card details.

4. Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@garageclean.ca.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated at any time. The current version on our website governs all future interactions.
