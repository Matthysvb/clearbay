Policies

Privacy Policy

Your information stays with us. That's it.

What We Collect

When you fill out a quote form or get in touch, we receive whatever you send us — typically your name, phone number, email address, and your property address so we can assess the job. We don't ask for anything we don't actually need to do our work.

What We Do With It

  • Get back to you with a quote
  • Schedule and confirm your service
  • Send reminders and follow up after the job
  • Improve how we operate

That's the list. We don't sell your information. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't pass it to third parties for any purpose outside delivering your service.

Keeping Your Data Safe

We take basic, sensible precautions with what you send us. Payment processing goes through secure third-party systems — we never see or store your full card number.

How Long We Keep It

We hold onto your information as long as we need it to service your account and meet any legal obligations. If you want your data deleted, email us and we'll remove it.

Reach Us

Privacy question or request? Email us at hello@garageclean.ca and we'll respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this from time to time. Whatever's published here is what governs — no retroactive changes, no surprises.
