About GarageClean

We make life easier, one garage at a time.

The garage is the room that gets promised, postponed, and ignored for years. It's too big to do in an hour. Too overwhelming to know where to start. And every weekend there's something that takes priority. That's the gap GarageClean was built to fill.

We serve busy homeowners across the North Shore and Burnaby — dual-income families who don't have a weekend to waste, downsizing couples dealing with decades of accumulation, and West Van homeowners with large properties that need a proper crew, not a single person with a pickup truck.

We show up, do the whole job, and leave the space better than you've seen it in years. Fully insured, flat-quoted, and locally run. No subcontractors, no surprises.
